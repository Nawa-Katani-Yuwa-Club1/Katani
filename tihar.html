Tihar

Tihar is the second biggest Nepalese festival after Dashain.Tihar, also called as Diwali or Deepawali, ranks second only to Dashain in Nepal.It is considered to be of great importance as it shows contribution to not just the humans and the gods, but also to the animals like crows, cows, and dogs that maintain an intimate relationship with humans. Tihar is also well-known as the festival of lights. There are different days in there. They are:-

Day 1 Kaag Tihar/Crow Day, worshiping crows

Day 2 Kukur Tihar/Dog Day, thanking dogs

Day 3 Gai Tihar and Laxmi Puja, honoring cow and the goddess of wealth

Day 4 Goru Puja, Govardhan Puja and Maha Puja, celebrating Newari New Year

Day 5 Bhai Tika
